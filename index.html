About Us

HikeIt.ie was founded with the intention of providing a place where hikers, mountaineers and hill walkers can trade stories and review lesser known trails. We are interested in the trails that we've never heard of. For such a small island, Ireland offers a huge variety of landscapes to explore. And nowhere is too far from the start point of a good walking route. My name is Liam Carroll and I've been exploring Irelands lesser known trails for over a decade. So welcome and please check out our trail and gear reviews, and post a trail review on our community section if you've just come off the mountain.
